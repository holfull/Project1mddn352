Proposal

The CliMine responds to the challenge set by MetService. The CliMine is different for every user of the app. The user simply types in their desired location and the CliMine at that location will display weather information in real time with the option to see a live video. It is personal because each user would be looking up different locations and at different times of the day, therefore the weather wouldn't be the same for every user.

The CliMine aims to provide it's users with real time weather information at the simple touch of a button. It's geometric design and industrial, waterproof plastic casing allows for certain weather conditions to not affect the camera and hardwiring inside. Along with allowing that no water builds up in one area of the device.

Breakdown of the CliMine

Description

The CliMine may look like your ordinary beacon that you could see around town but it does a whole lot more than tell your phone where you are. The CliMine is a weather application and object that you can have on your andriod, i-phone or any other smartphone. It’s sleek design and option to have different colour casings makes it the perfect object for any business to want to have.

How it Works

How it Works

Inside the CliMine are different weather sensors and a camera that gives you real-time weather information straight to the app. The sensors are working 24/7 and the CliMine casing protects the hardwiring. You simply type in the location you’re wanting to get weather information about, click launch and all the information is right in your hand. Tap on the weather icon and you can see a live stream from the camera in the CliMine.
